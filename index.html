CONNECT X

Connecting the dots across Web · Flutter · AI

About

Connect X is a nonprofit student activity based in the Faculty of Computer and Information Sciences at Ain Shams University. Guided by our slogan, “Connecting the Dots”, we strive to bridge the gap between university education and real market needs by equipping students with both knowledge and hands-on experience. At our core, we are a vibrant community of students passionate about technology, creativity, and collaboration. Through diverse committees—ranging from AI and Web to HR, Media, PR, and beyond—we create a dynamic space for learning, experimenting, and building impactful projects together.

Vision

In the next decade, Connect X aspires to become a fully integrated educational academy that empowers students to acquire practical skills. Our long-term goal is to see a startup founded by an ex-ConnectX president or ex-member, leveraging the knowledge, experience, and growth we've gained through our community, and to recruit other members of our family to create more solutions and drive growth.

Mission

  • Push boundaries through innovation and collaboration.
  • Build two cross-committee projects to simulate real corporate teamwork.
  • Host a large-scale Connect X Hackathon for students to showcase skills.
